About Me

My name is Ella Kowaleski and I am a sophomore at the University of South Carolina. I am majoring in psychology and minoring in biology. I am from a small town in Wisconsin but decided to come all the way down here to South Carolina to get away from the people in my hometown. I was labeled a shy, quiet, girl in high school and I didn't want that to come into college with me. Nobody ever came down here, so it seemed like the perfect decision for me, and so far it has been!

I am a part of the Kappa Gamma chapter of the sorority Alpha Xi Delta. I am the Circle of Sisterhood Delegate for my chapter as well. I am also a part of the Fundraising Committee for USC's Relay For Life team. Outside of school work, I enjoy going out with friends, having movie nights, and relaxing by myself. A downside of living so far away is that I don't get to see my family often. I am really close with both my parents as well as my younger sister and brother. Another downside, I don't get to see my dog often either. She's a labrador retriever named Raven and she has my whole heart.

My Future Career Plans

My future plans include becoming an Animal Physical Therapist, specifically dogs. This means finishing undergraduate a semester early and then going to graduate school, specifically Physical Therapy. I have not decided quite yet where I want to go to graduate school, my choices so far include; South Carolina, Wisconsin, or somewhere out west like Colorado or Montana. My thought process is that I only live once, so while I'm young with not many commitments, I should try to go everywhere possible. I might take a gap year between undergrad and grad school so I can be a traveling pet sitter but that is still up in the air. After grad school, I will go to Tennessee to get certified to work on animals.

After all my schooling is done, I will move back to my hometown. I think I'm going to need to work in a hospital as a Physical Therapist on humans for a little bit to save up some money. When I have enough money saved, then I will open my own Physical Therapy clinic for dogs. If my grandma will be ready to sell her house, I will want to buy it because it has a barn where I can convert it to my business area. There will always be a dog in my life, I'm hoping after undergrad, but I plan on adopting a dog every 5 years.
